Specifications

  • 12x9x3cm water-resistant hardware enclosure with various sensors, such as a camera and GNSS receiver, hosts AI and AR cutting-edge algorithms.

  • You can find your way, avoid dangerous water, and reach your destination in the most efficient way.

  • This device connects to your personal phone, tablet, boat dashboard, or our laptop, and you can see everything augmented on the real-time camera footage.

The camera should also point towards the front of your boat and view the sea. We need to know your boat's draft and your desired clearance beforehand.
